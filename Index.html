Controls

  • Move - A/D or Left/Right
  • Jump - Space
  • Sprint - Shift
  • Pause - P
  • Blue Portal - Left Click
  • Red Portal - Shift-Left Click

Features

  • Multiple characters.
  • Full collision detection.
  • Ability to sprint.
  • Pause menu.
  • Ability to Die with menu.
  • Tracks number of deaths.
  • Ability to win with menu.
  • Easy map editing (see Map1.js).
  • Working portals.
